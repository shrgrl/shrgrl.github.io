Your privacy is just as important as you are. This policy helps you feel more secure while browsing my site. I work to know as little as possible about you and ensure that any information you share remains confidential.

Website Analytics

I use Google Analytics to better understand who my website visitors are and where they come from. This helps me improve the user experience and content relevance. All data collected is anonymized and used solely for analytical purposes.

Contact Form Information

When you use the contact form on my website, estimated geographic information may be added to help me better understand my audience. I maintain strict security and utmost confidentiality with all information you provide. Using the contact form is entirely optional.

Data Protection Principles

  • All personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently
  • Data collection is limited to what's necessary for legitimate purposes
  • Information is kept accurate and up-to-date
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary
  • Your information is processed securely with appropriate technical measures

Policy Updates

I may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revision date clearly indicated.
