What Should We Do?

Years passed quickly and I graduated with what could be considered an ideal average. But let me also mention this: when applying for jobs, no one cares what average you graduated with. The real issue is how much you can develop yourself and how much you can show it. First, question what areas give you pleasure. Of course, we may not only do jobs we love in the business world, but if we develop ourselves in the field we want, employers will also evaluate us in the direction we have developed. Never put aside what you learned at school, your assignments, your projects. They will all find a place in your resume as experiences. Also, uploading all your projects to your Github account in an organized way will provide convenience for people to examine and will be an indicator of you being a more organized person. With these thoughts, I first decided to create a platform where I could clearly express myself and created my own website, developed different projects. I read, researched, and I'm still continuing. If you're entering this profession, the first rule for me is to be enthusiastic. If you do your job with enthusiasm and love, patience, dedication and desire follow. For a person who has all these, success becomes an inevitable end.

The foundation language of programming languages is English. And unfortunately, the education we received in public high school is not sufficient to deal with these. It's very difficult to find an error you encounter in a program from Turkish sources. It's very important to improve your English and do your research from the right sources.
